Learn Node.js with a group in a casual environment!

Node School Fort Worth is a monthly meetup to help people learn Node, JavaScript and other programming languages. Each meetup will focus on a workshopper from Node School. Our FTW mentors will be teaching and available to help out through-out each class. Whether you're just starting out or have been developing for years, our workshops can help you learn something new or strengthen your knowledge!
